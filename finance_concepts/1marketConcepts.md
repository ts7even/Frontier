## Time Value of Money

- Present Value
    $$ PV = {FV \over (1 + {r \over n} )^{t * n}} $$

- Future Value
    $$ FV = {PV * (1 + {r \over n} )^{t * n}} $$

- Annuities
    $$ PV of Annuities = PMT *  {{[1 - (1 + {r \over n} )^{-t*n}}] \over {r \over n}}  $$ 


    $$ FV of Annuities = PMT *  {{[(1+{r \over n})^{t*n} -1}] \over {r \over n}}  $$
